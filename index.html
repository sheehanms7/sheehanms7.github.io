About Grand Theft Auto

"Grand Theft Auto" is a groundbreaking video game franchise that revolutionized the gaming industry with its open-world design, immersive gameplay, and controversial themes. Developed by Rockstar Games, it places players in sprawling, dynamic environments where they can engage in various criminal activities, from car theft to organized crime. Known for its gritty storytelling, vast landscapes, and satirical take on modern society, "Grand Theft Auto" has captivated millions of players worldwide since its inception in 1997. It has left an indelible mark on gaming culture, sparking debates about violence in video games and pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
